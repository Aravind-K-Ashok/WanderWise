Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to pay upfront?

No. You only pay once you're satisfied with your custom travel plan.

2. Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can make changes anytime before your travel date.

3. Is support available during the trip?

Absolutely. Our support team is available 24/7 throughout your journey.

4. How do I get the best deals?

We constantly track prices and offer discounts on flights, hotels, and packages tailored to your preferences.

5. What if I need to cancel my trip?

You can cancel your trip at any time. Refunds depend on the policies of the service providers we book with, and we’ll assist you with the process.

6. Are the travel plans personalized?

Yes, every travel plan is tailored to your interests, preferences, and budget. You have full control over the itinerary.

7. Do you book flights and accommodations?

Yes, we handle bookings for flights, accommodations, activities, and even transportation if needed.

8. How long does it take to get my plan?

Typically, you’ll receive your custom travel plan within 24–48 hours after providing your preferences.

9. Is there a mobile app I can use?

Yes, our mobile app allows you to view your itinerary, get real-time updates, and contact support while you travel.

10. Can I plan a trip for a group?

Absolutely! We offer custom plans for solo travelers, couples, families, and large groups with shared or individual preferences.
