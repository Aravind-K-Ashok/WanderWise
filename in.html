Wayanad

Wayanad is a picturesque district in Kerala, India, known for its lush green landscapes, rolling hills, and rich biodiversity. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it is a haven for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. The region is characterized by its coffee and spice plantations, dense forests, and serene waterfalls.

Alappey

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a picturesque town in Kerala, India, famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises. It is often referred to as the "Venice of the East" due to its intricate network of canals and lagoons. The town is situated on the Arabian Sea coast and is renowned for its scenic beauty, lush green landscapes, and vibrant culture.

Munnar
Name

Munnar is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Idukki district of Kerala, India. Known for its rolling hills blanketed with lush tea plantations, Munnar sits at the confluence of three mountain streams—Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni, and Kundala. The town is situated at an altitude of around 1,600 meters above sea level, offering a cool and refreshing climate throughout the year, making it a popular retreat for travelers seeking respite from the heat of the plains.

Ooty, nestled in the Nilgiri Hills, is a serene hill station known for its lush tea gardens, cool climate, and picturesque landscapes.
Ooty

Ooty is a charming hill station nestled in the Nilgiri district of Tamil Nadu, India. Famed for its verdant landscapes and expansive tea estates, Ooty is set amidst rolling hills and dense forests. Perched at an elevation of about 2,240 meters above sea level, it enjoys a cool, temperate climate year-round, making it a favored getaway for those looking to escape the warmth of the lowlands.
